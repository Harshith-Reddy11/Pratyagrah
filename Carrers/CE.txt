Civil Engineer:

Career Description: Designs and oversees the construction of infrastructure projects like bridges, buildings, roads, dams, and water treatment systems. They ensure safety, functionality, and adherence to building codes and regulations. Civil engineers use engineering principles, computer-aided design (CAD) software, and project management skills to bring infrastructure projects to life.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $97,380

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 6% (Slower than average)
