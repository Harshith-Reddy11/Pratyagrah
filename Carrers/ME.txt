Mechanical Engineer:

Career Description: Mechanical engineers design, develop, and test mechanical systems and devices. They use engineering principles, computer-aided design (CAD) software, and other tools to create solutions for various applications, from machines and engines to robotics and medical equipment. This role requires a strong foundation in physics, mathematics, and engineering principles.

Career Salary: $99,640

Career Growth Rate: 7% (As fast as average) - Mechanical engineers are involved in a wide range of industries, ensuring consistent job demand.
