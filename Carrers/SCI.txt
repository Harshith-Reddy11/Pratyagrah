Scientist:

Career Description: Conducts research in various scientific fields like biology, chemistry, physics, or environmental science. Scientists may specialize in specific areas, conduct experiments, analyze data, and develop new knowledge or technologies. They require strong analytical skills, problem-solving abilities, and a passion for scientific inquiry.
