Fitness Trainer:

Career Description: Motivates and guides individuals to achieve their fitness goals through personalized exercise programs. Fitness trainers assess clients' needs, design workout routines, provide instruction on proper exercise techniques, and track progress. They may work in gyms, fitness centers, personal training studios, or offer freelance services.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $40,090

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 14% (Faster than average) - The increasing focus on health and wellness is creating a growing demand for fitness trainers.
