Business Analyst:

Career Description: Analyzes business processes, data, and systems to identify areas for improvement. They develop recommendations to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and enhance profitability. Business analysts use data analysis techniques, communication skills, and problem-solving abilities to help businesses achieve their goals.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $93,730

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 10% (Faster than average)
