CEO (Chief Executive Officer):

Career Description: Leads and manages the overall operations of a company. CEOs set the strategic direction, oversee daily operations, make major decisions, and represent the company to stakeholders. They require strong leadership, business acumen, and a deep understanding of the industry.

Career Salary: Highly Variable (Depends on company size, industry, and performance)

Career Growth Rate: Not Applicable (Typically achieved through experience and career progression)
