 Pilot:

Career Description: Operates commercial airplanes, cargo planes, or private aircraft. Pilots navigate aircraft, ensure safety for passengers and cargo, and follow air traffic regulations. They may specialize in commercial airlines, private aviation, or freight transportation.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $132,120

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 5% (Slower than average)
