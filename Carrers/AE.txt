 Aerospace Engineer:

Career Description: Develops and designs aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems. They use engineering principles, computer software, and advanced technologies to create solutions for various aerospace applications. Responsibilities may involve aerodynamics, propulsion systems, structural integrity, and flight testing.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $135,990

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 8% (As fast as average)
