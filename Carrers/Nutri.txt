Nutritionist:

Career Description: Advises individuals on healthy eating habits for weight management, disease prevention, or managing specific health conditions. Nutritionists assess dietary needs, create personalized meal plans, educate clients on nutrition principles, and provide guidance on making healthy food choices. They may work in hospitals, clinics, public health departments, or private practice.

Career Salary: (Median Annual Wage: May 2023) $63,080

Career Growth Rate: (Projected 2022-2032) 9% (Faster than average) - The growing awareness of the link between diet and health is increasing the demand for qualified nutritionists.
